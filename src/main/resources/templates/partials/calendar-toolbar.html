{! Shared calendar toolbar — a single, non-wrapping row reused for EVERY calendar style (full, minimal, stacked) so the navigation chrome is identical across calendars, themes and viewports. Three groups: the prev-year/prev-month buttons (« ‹) are pinned to the LEFT edge, the next-month/next-year buttons (› ») to the RIGHT edge, and the month/year title + jump-to-month glyph sit centred between them (`justify-content: space-between` with equal-width side groups keeps the centre group dead-centre). dashboard.html wires the stable button ids to the shared grid engine (buildGridCalendar) that draws every style — full, minimal and stacked. The "Today" action lives at the bottom of the month/year picker popup (#cal-pop), NOT in the toolbar row. The popup is positioned absolutely against the enclosing `#calendar-wrap` (which is `position: relative`). !}
